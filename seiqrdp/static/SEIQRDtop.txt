<H2>SEIR Model with Partial Quarantine of Infectives<br>and Interaction Reduction</H2><p>P = Not Susceptible (e.g. protected by vaccine)<br>S = Susceptible (not yet exposed),<br> E = Exposed (infected, but not yet able to infect others),<br> I = Infective (able to infect others), <br>Q = Quarantined (removed from the Infective general population),<br>R = Recovered (no longer infective, either survived or deceased).<p>N (population size) set at 10000 = P + S + E + I + Q + R<br><br><br><br><br>
